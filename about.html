About Me

Hello! I'm Suril Pokharel, a passionate Web Developer, UI/UX Designer, and Creative Thinker. My goal is to turn ideas into visually stunning, functional, and user-friendly digital experiences. I take pride in building websites that are not only efficient but also engaging and accessible.

I started my journey in the world of technology driven by curiosity and creativity. Over time, I have honed my skills in front-end and back-end development, mastering tools like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and frameworks like Tailwind CSS and React.js. My design approach focuses on simplicity, balance, and a smooth user journey.

Beyond coding, I’m deeply interested in content creation, brand storytelling, and visual identity design. I believe that the best designs communicate powerful messages and connect emotionally with the audience. I enjoy working on collaborative projects that challenge me to think differently and grow both creatively and technically.

I’m currently open to freelance opportunities, collaborations, and new projects where I can contribute my skills and continue learning from talented individuals around the world. If you’re looking for a passionate developer who values design, performance, and innovation — I’d love to connect.
